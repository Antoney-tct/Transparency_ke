Nairobi-Mombasa Expressway

Location: Nairobi to Mombasa, Kenya Started: 2022 Expected Completion: 2026
Project Progress
35%
Construction Ongoing

The Nairobi-Mombasa Expressway is a transformative 473km highway project that will connect Kenya's two largest cities, significantly improving transportation and boosting economic activity along the corridor. This modern toll road will be the country's most advanced highway when completed.

The expressway features four lanes with a design speed of 120km/h, multiple interchanges, service areas, and modern safety features. It is designed to reduce travel time between Nairobi and Mombasa from the current 10+ hours to just 4.5 hours, while improving safety and reliability.

The project is being implemented through a Public-Private Partnership (PPP) with an estimated cost of KSh 300 billion. It will feature electronic toll collection and is expected to create over 6,000 direct jobs during construction while stimulating economic growth along the entire corridor.
