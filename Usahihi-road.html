About the Project
Usahihi Road — Machakos

Usahihi Road is a county-level road rehabilitation project in Machakos County, near the town of Masii. The project upgrades existing rural and semi-urban access routes to all-weather tarmac standards, serving surrounding agricultural and residential communities.


The project aims to open agricultural corridors, reduce post-harvest losses caused by inaccessible roads, and improve emergency response access. Once complete, the road will significantly reduce journey times and transport costs for thousands of residents in the Masii and Mwala sub-counties.
