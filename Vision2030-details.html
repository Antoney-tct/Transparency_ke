Kenya Vision 2030

Launched: June 2008 Timeframe: 2008-2030 Status: Ongoing
Implementation Progress
72%
Mid-Term Phase

Kenya Vision 2030 is the national long-term development blueprint that aims to transform Kenya into a newly industrializing, middle-income country providing a high quality of life to all its citizens by 2030. The vision is anchored on three key pillars:

Economic Pillar

Targeting sustained economic growth of 10% annually through key sectors like tourism, agriculture, manufacturing, and business process outsourcing.

Social Pillar

Focusing on education, health, water and sanitation, housing, and gender equality to build a just and cohesive society.

Political Pillar

Based on democratic principles, rule of law, and people's participation in governance through devolution.

The Vision is being implemented through successive 5-year Medium Term Plans, with the current phase (MTP IV) covering 2023-2027.
