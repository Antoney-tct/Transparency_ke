Primary School Textbook Distribution

Department: Education | Status: Completed | Budget: Ksh2.1M

Project Summary

This initiative successfully delivered textbooks to all 320 primary schools in the state, ensuring every student in grades 1-6 had access to required learning materials for the 2023-2024 academic year.

Key Achievements

  • Delivered 450,000 textbooks across 6 subjects
  • 100% of schools received materials before start of term
  • Included special education materials for 5,000 students with disabilities
  • Provided digital versions to all schools

Impact Assessment

The project has resulted in:

  • Elimination of textbook shortages in all primary schools
  • Improved student performance (15% increase in test scores)
  • Reduced burden on parents (saving an estimated $35 per child)
