eCitizen — Digital Gateway

Kenya's eCitizen platform is Africa's most advanced government digital services portal, providing 5,000+ services from over 60 government agencies. In 2024 alone, over KSh 18 billion in government revenues was collected through eCitizen, eliminating queues and corruption at service counters.
