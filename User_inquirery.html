Tips for a good inquiry

01
Be specific — name the project, location, or ministry you are asking about.
02
Use a valid email — responses are sent directly to your inbox.
03
Attach reference numbers or dates where available for faster processing.
04
Keep your message factual and respectful for the best outcome.
