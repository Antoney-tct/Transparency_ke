About Nairobi County

Nairobi, Kenya's vibrant capital city, is the only city in the world with a national park hosting diverse wildlife. As the economic and political hub of East Africa, Nairobi County is committed to delivering efficient services, fostering economic growth, and improving the quality of life for all residents.

Our county government works tirelessly to ensure sustainable urban development, quality healthcare, education, and infrastructure while preserving our unique natural heritage.
