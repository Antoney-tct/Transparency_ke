Construction Phase

The Affordable Housing Program (AHP) is a flagship project under Kenya's Big Four Agenda, aiming to deliver 500,000 affordable homes by 2027 to address the nation's housing deficit estimated at 2 million units.

Program Objectives

  • Provide decent housing for low and middle-income earners
  • Create 350,000+ direct jobs in construction
  • Stimulate local manufacturing of building materials
  • Develop integrated communities with social amenities

Unit Categories

Social Housing: KSh 600K - 1.5M
Affordable Housing: KSh 1M - 3M
Mortgage Gap: KSh 3M - 6M

The program offers Boma Yangu tenant purchase scheme with 2.5% mortgage rates and flexible payment plans.
