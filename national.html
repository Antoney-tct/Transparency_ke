About the Government of Kenya

The Government of Kenya is committed to serving all citizens with integrity, transparency, and efficiency. Our national institutions work together to implement policies, deliver services, and drive sustainable development across all sectors of the economy.

This portal provides centralized access to government services, official information, and resources to empower citizens and promote public participation in governance. We strive to be an open, accountable government that serves all Kenyans equally.
