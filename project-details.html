About the Project
Talanta Sports Stadium

The Talanta Stadium is a flagship government investment in sports infrastructure, located along Ngong Road in Nairobi. Designed to be a world-class, multi-purpose venue, the stadium will host international football matches, athletics events, concerts, and national ceremonies.


The facility is designed with a retractable roof, state-of-the-art LED lighting, and premium hospitality suites. Its name — Talanta (Swahili for "talent") — reflects the government's commitment to nurturing Kenya's sporting and artistic talent at the highest level.

Key Features
What's Being Built
  • 60,000-seat capacity with VIP hospitality suites
  • Retractable hybrid roof system
  • FIFA-certified artificial + natural hybrid turf
  • 4K scoreboards and broadcast infrastructure
  • Velodrome for cycling events
  • Indoor arena (5,000 capacity) attached
  • Underground parking for 3,000 vehicles
  • Public park and recreational spaces
