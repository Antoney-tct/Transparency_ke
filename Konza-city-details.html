Konza Technopolis

Location: Machakos County, Kenya Started: January 2013 Expected Completion: Phase 1 by 2025
Project Progress
65%
Phase 1 Ongoing

Konza Technopolis is Kenya's flagship smart city project, envisioned as a world-class technology hub and major economic driver for the nation. Located 60km southeast of Nairobi, the 5,000-acre development is part of Kenya's Vision 2030 economic development blueprint.

The city is being developed in four phases, with Phase 1 focusing on core infrastructure including roads, water systems, and the Konza Complex. When complete, it will host business process outsourcing, software development, data centers, and research institutions, creating over 20,000 jobs in its first phase.

Konza Technopolis is being developed as a sustainable smart city with green energy solutions, smart mobility systems, and digital infrastructure to support Kenya's growing tech ecosystem and position the country as Africa's Silicon Savannah.
