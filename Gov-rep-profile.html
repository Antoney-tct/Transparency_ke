Official Biography
About the Official
Hon. Antoney Citizen serves as Cabinet Secretary overseeing fiscal policy, public debt management, and economic strategy for the Republic of Kenya. With over a decade of experience in public finance, the CS has championed transparency, budget discipline, and data-driven governance.

Prior to this appointment, they served as a senior economist and adviser to the Kenya Revenue Authority, contributing to landmark tax reforms that increased the tax-to-GDP ratio by 2 percentage points over five years.
