Our Progress Timeline

2008
Vision 2030 Launched

Kenya's long-term development blueprint was officially launched, setting the country's development agenda.

2010
New Constitution

The new constitution was promulgated, marking a significant milestone in Kenya's governance structure.

2012
Thika Superhighway

The completed Thika Superhighway was commissioned, revolutionizing transport in the Nairobi metropolitan area.

2017
Standard Gauge Railway

The first phase of the SGR was launched, connecting Mombasa to Nairobi.

2022
Digital Transformation

Major government services were digitized through the eCitizen platform.

2025
Universal Healthcare

Projected completion of universal healthcare coverage rollout across all counties.
