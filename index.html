Milestones

Our Progress Timeline

From Vision 2030's launch to today — key moments in Kenya's development journey.

2008
Vision 2030 Launched

Kenya's long-term development blueprint was officially launched, setting the national development agenda for decades.

2010
New Constitution

A new constitution was promulgated, marking a significant milestone in Kenya's governance and devolution structure.

2012
Thika Superhighway

The completed Thika Superhighway was commissioned, revolutionising transport in the Nairobi metropolitan area.

2017
Standard Gauge Railway

Phase one of the SGR was launched, connecting Mombasa to Nairobi and transforming national logistics.

2022
Digital Transformation

Major government services were digitised through the eCitizen platform, improving public access.

2025
Universal Healthcare

Projected completion of universal healthcare coverage rollout across all 47 counties.
