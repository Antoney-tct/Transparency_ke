Construction of Regional Hospital

TENDER #GOV-2023-0451 | Department: Health | Budget: $12.5M

Project Description

Construction of a 200-bed regional hospital in the Northern District, including:

  • Main hospital building with emergency, surgical, and inpatient facilities
  • Outpatient clinic and diagnostic center
  • Staff housing and administrative offices
  • Parking for 300 vehicles
  • Landscaping and external works

Key Dates

Published: April 15, 2023

Deadline for Questions: May 30, 2023

Submission Deadline: June 15, 2023

Evaluation Period: June 16 - July 15, 2023

Expected Award Date: July 30, 2023

Eligibility Requirements

  • Minimum 5 years experience in healthcare facility construction
  • Valid construction license
  • Proof of financial capability
  • Compliance with all safety and environmental regulations

School Textbook Procurement

TENDER #GOV-2023-0382 | Department: Education | Budget: $2.1M

Project Description

Procurement and distribution of textbooks for primary and secondary schools across the state for the 2023-2024 academic year:

  • Mathematics (Grades 1-12)
  • Science (Grades 1-12)
  • Language Arts (Grades 1-12)
  • Social Studies (Grades 4-12)
  • Special Education materials

Key Dates

Published: March 28, 2023

Deadline for Questions: May 15, 2023

Submission Deadline: May 30, 2023

Expected Delivery Date: August 1, 2023

Technical Specifications

  • Books must meet national curriculum standards
  • Durable binding and high-quality paper
  • Delivery to 250 schools statewide
  • Digital versions required for all titles

Highway Maintenance Services

TENDER #GOV-2023-0427 | Department: Transport | Budget: $8.75M

Service Requirements

Three-year contract for routine maintenance of 150km of state highways including:

  • Pothole repairs and pavement maintenance
  • Road signage and markings
  • Drainage system maintenance
  • Shoulder maintenance
  • Winter snow and ice control
  • Emergency response within 2 hours

Key Dates

Published: May 5, 2023

Site Visits: May 20-25, 2023

Submission Deadline: July 5, 2023

Contract Start Date: September 1, 2023

Evaluation Criteria

  • Price (40%)
  • Experience with similar projects (30%)
  • Equipment and personnel capacity (20%)
  • Local employment commitment (10%)
