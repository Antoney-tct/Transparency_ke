Our Vision Statement

The Kenya Vision 2030 is the country's development blueprint covering the period 2008 to 2030. It aims to transform Kenya into a newly industrializing, middle-income country providing a high quality of life to all its citizens in a clean and secure environment.

"A globally competitive and prosperous nation with a high quality of life by 2030"

This vision is based on three key pillars: Economic, Social, and Political Governance. It is a people-centered, private sector-led strategy that seeks to create a globally competitive and adaptive economy that meets the needs of all Kenyans.

The Three Pillars

Economic Pillar

Seeks to achieve an average economic growth rate of 10% per annum from 2012 to 2030 through:

  • Tourism development
  • Agriculture and livestock
  • Wholesale and retail trade
  • Manufacturing for regional market
  • Business process outsourcing
  • Financial services

Social Pillar

Aims to build a just and cohesive society with social equity in a clean and secure environment through:

  • Education and training
  • Health system strengthening
  • Water and sanitation
  • Environment and natural resources
  • Housing and urbanization
  • Gender, youth and vulnerable groups

Political Pillar

Seeks to realize a democratic political system founded on issue-based politics through:

  • Rule of law
  • Electoral and political processes
  • Democracy and public service delivery
  • Transparency and accountability
  • Security, peace building and conflict management
