Cabinet History
Ruto Cabinet Reshuffles — 2022 to 2025
September 2022
First Cabinet Appointed
Ruto appoints 22 Cabinet Secretaries after winning the August 2022 elections. Includes Kindiki (Interior), Duale (Defence), Mudavadi (Prime CS), Chirchir (Energy).
July 11, 2024
Entire Cabinet Dismissed — Gen Z Protests
Following the Finance Bill Gen Z protests, Ruto dismisses his entire cabinet — except Musalia Mudavadi (Prime CS) and Foreign Affairs. All ministries temporarily vacant. Major political reset.
November 1, 2024
Kithure Kindiki Sworn as Deputy President
Following impeachment of Rigathi Gachagua by National Assembly (Oct 8) and Senate (Oct 17), Kithure Kindiki is sworn as Kenya's 3rd Deputy President, vacating the Interior CS position.
January 17, 2025
Three New CSs Appointed
Mutahi Kagwe (Agriculture), Lee Kinyanjui (Trade & Industry), and William Kabogo Gitau (ICT & Digital Economy) appointed — replacing former Gachagua allies in the broad-based cabinet.
March 26, 2025
CS Justin Muturi Fired
Public Service CS Justin Muturi fired after silent battles with President Ruto over his push to discuss Gen Z protests. Mbeere North MP Geoffrey Ruku nominated as replacement. Aden Duale moved to Health; Deborah Barasa takes Environment.
