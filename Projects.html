Kenya Vision 2030

Kenya's development blueprint aims to transform the country into a globally competitive, middle-income nation by 2030. Every project listed on this portal contributes to one or more of the Vision's three pillars.

This portal tracks progress across all pillars to ensure transparent accountability in implementation for every Kenyan citizen.

Political Pillar

Nurturing issue-based, transparent and accountable democratic governance
